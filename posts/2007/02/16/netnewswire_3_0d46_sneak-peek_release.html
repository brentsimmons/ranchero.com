@dbid 1560 @title NetNewsWire 3.0d46: sneak-peek release @link http://ranchero.com/netnewswire/beta.php @pubDate Fri Feb 16 15:00:28 -0800 2007 @modDate Fri Feb 16 15:01:58 -0800 2007 @categoryArray NetNewsWire, NetNewsWire-Updates We just posted a sneak-peek release of NetNewsWire 3 today. It’s not even a beta yet, and it has bugs, unfinished features, unstarted features, and it still needs more polish and testing. All that said, you can download it from the beta page. ;)

The change notes page lists what’s new since 2.1.1. Highlights include Spotlight searching, new Combined View, feed ‘cover art’, full screen mode, Growl notifications, tabs with thumbnails and animations, the ability to choose a folder when subscribing, hiding/showing the subscriptions list, and lots more.
