@dbid 1652 @title Twitterrific 2.1 @link http://iconfactory.com/software/twitterrific/ @pubDate Fri Jun 08 21:42:43 -0700 2007 @modDate Fri Jun 08 21:42:43 -0700 2007 Iconfactory’s Twitterrific 2.1 is out. If you have Twitterrific 2.1 installed and NetNewsWire 3.x, then, when you’re reading something cool and you want to tweet it, you can hit ctrl-1 or choose Send to Twitterrific from the News menu.

Then Twitterrific puts some text like “Reading [some page title] at [some page URL]” in the text area, which you can edit (if you want) before posting.

It’s a lot like how the send-to-weblog feature works. (In fact, under the hood, it uses the exact same Apple events interface.)
