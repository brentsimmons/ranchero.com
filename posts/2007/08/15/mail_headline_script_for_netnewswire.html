@dbid 1686 @title Mail Headline script for NetNewsWire @link http://raoli.com/2007/08/13/mail-headline/ @pubDate Wed Aug 15 19:36:46 -0700 2007 @modDate Wed Aug 15 19:36:46 -0700 2007 @categoryArray NetNewsWire, NetNewsWire-Scripts raoli.com: “Mail Headline is a plain-text replacement for the Mail Contents of this News Item command that’s capable of supporting multiple email clients.
