@dbid 1394 @title Standing in Line With Mr. Jimmy @link http://daringfireball.net/2006/07/mr_jimmy @pubDate Sat Jul 15 10:43:43 -0700 2006 @modDate Sat Jul 15 10:43:43 -0700 2006 Daring Fireball: “Mac OS X is a very hackable platform; it just isn’t hackable by means of the raw source code to the software. This is one area where Mac OS X inherits good aspects from both sides of its family. From the classic Mac OS, it inherits AppleScript and a tradition of rich plug-in APIs for major applications. From Next, it inherits both the entire (highly scriptable) Unix layer and the eminently hackable and modular Objective-C runtime system.”
