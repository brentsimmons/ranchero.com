@dbid 223 @title The Evolution of Darwin @link http://developer.apple.com/darwin/history.html @pubDate Tue May 14 10:13:40 -0700 2002 @modDate Tue May 14 10:13:40 -0700 2002 Apple: “Darwin is like Linux with a day job: By day, it stays discreetly in the background, running Mac OS X. Users are happy, but engineers also get to have their fun.”
