@dbid 215 @title CSS Beyond the Browser: Going to Print @link http://www.alistapart.com/stories/goingtoprint/ @pubDate Fri May 10 09:41:08 -0700 2002 @modDate Fri May 10 09:41:08 -0700 2002 A List Apart: “You’ve seen them before: links that say ‘click here for printer-friendly version’ or words to that effect. Every time you follow one of those links, you load up a separate document that presents exactly the same information with a different layout, and probably different markup. That means somebody (or a script) had to take the original document and convert it to a stripped-down version that’s more suitable for print output. Maybe that somebody was even you.”
