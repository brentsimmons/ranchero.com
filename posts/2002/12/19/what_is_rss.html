@dbid 574 @title What is RSS? @link http://www.xml.com/pub/a/2002/12/18/dive-into-xml.html @pubDate Thu Dec 19 10:20:03 -0800 2002 @modDate Thu Dec 19 10:20:03 -0800 2002 Mark Pilgrim writes for XML.com: “It’s not just for news. Pretty much anything that can be broken down into discrete items can be syndicated via RSS: the ‘recent changes’ page of a wiki, a changelog of CVS checkins, even the revision history of a book. Once information about each item is in RSS format, an RSS-aware program can check the feed for changes and react to the changes in an appropriate way.”
