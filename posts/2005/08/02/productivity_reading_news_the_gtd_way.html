@dbid 1157 @title Productivity: Reading News The GTD Way @link http://dmiessler.com/archives/416 @pubDate Tue Aug 02 16:18:20 -0700 2005 @modDate Tue Aug 02 16:18:20 -0700 2005 @categoryArray NetNewsWire, NetNewsWire-Tips Daniel Miessler describes his system for efficiently reading news using an RSS aggregator and a browser with tabs, based on David Allen's Getting Things Done.
