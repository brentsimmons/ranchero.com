@dbid 1076 @title Keyboard shortcut for searching @link @pubDate Tue Apr 05 19:20:14 -0700 2005 @modDate Tue Apr 05 19:20:14 -0700 2005 @categoryArray NetNewsWire, NetNewsWire-Tips To put the cursor in the search field in the toolbar, type cmd-opt-F. (If the search field is visible in the toolbar.)

Then to move focus back to the headlines table, type the Tab key.
