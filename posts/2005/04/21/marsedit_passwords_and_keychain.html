@dbid 1094 @title MarsEdit passwords and Keychain @link @pubDate Thu Apr 21 17:34:36 -0700 2005 @modDate Thu Apr 21 17:34:36 -0700 2005 @categoryArray MarsEdit, MarsEdit-Tips The weblog password used by MarsEdit is stored in Keychain so it remains there even if the weblog is deleted. However, you can manually delete it in Keychain Access.

1. Quit MarsEdit if it is running.
2. Launch Keychain Access (Applications/Utilities/Keychain Access)
3. Look for MarsEdit:[your username]
4. You can edit it within Keychain Access or delete this item and then MarsEdit will ask you for a new username/password when you recreate the weblog.
