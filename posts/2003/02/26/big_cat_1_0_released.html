@dbid 660 @title Big Cat 1.0 Released @link http://ranchero.com/bigcat/ @pubDate Wed Feb 26 14:53:25 -0800 2003 @modDate Wed Feb 26 14:54:34 -0800 2003 Big Cat is a freeware contextual menu plugin that adds a Scripts menu to apps like the Finder and BBEdit, apps that support system contextual menus. You can add your own scripts—AppleScript and shell scripts—to the Scripts menu.
