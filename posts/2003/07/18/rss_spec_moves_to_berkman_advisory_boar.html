@dbid 782 @title RSS Spec Moves to Berkman, Advisory Board Formed @link http://blogs.law.harvard.edu/tech/announceRss2 @pubDate Fri Jul 18 16:21:08 -0700 2003 @modDate Fri Jul 18 16:22:06 -0700 2003 UserLand has transferred ownership of its RSS 2.0 spec to the Berkman Center for Internet & Society at Harvard Law School. The spec is now licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution/Share Alike license. An advisory board was formed to support and advocate RSS. Its initial members are Dave Winer, Jon Udell, and Brent Simmons.
