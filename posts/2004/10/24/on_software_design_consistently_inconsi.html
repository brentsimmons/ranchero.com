@dbid 952 @title On Software Design: Consistently Inconsistent @link http://www.advenio.com/weblogs/2004/10/on-software-design-consistently.html @pubDate Sun Oct 24 23:55:58 -0700 2004 @modDate Sun Oct 24 23:55:58 -0700 2004 Michael Dupuis: “At first blush, you’d look at these two sets of applications and determine that ‘Aqua apps run to the window edges, and Metal apps have borders.’ But herein lies one of the many inconsistencies.
