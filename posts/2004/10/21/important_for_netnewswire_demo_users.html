@dbid 944 @title Important: for NetNewsWire Demo Users @link @pubDate Thu Oct 21 20:55:01 -0700 2004 @modDate Thu Oct 21 21:13:17 -0700 2004 If you’re using a demo version of NetNewsWire, and you think it’s anywhere near expiring—don’t quit the program. There is a bug in the beta where if you quit, then launch it after it’s expired, you could lose your flagged items and read/unread status.

We’re going to release a new beta that fixes this bug tomorrow. In the meantime, please accept our apologies.

You can tell when your demo expires by choosing License... from the NetNewsWire menu.
