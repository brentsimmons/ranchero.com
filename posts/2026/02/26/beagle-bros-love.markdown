Marcin Wichary reminds us, in [“Our programs are fun to use”](https://unsung.aresluna.org/our-programs-are-fun-to-use), of the great Beagle Bros, the inspiration for so many indie app developers — of indies who went on to inspire other indies. Without their leadership by example the indie app world would be smaller and far less colorful.
